Using Discount Codes and Special Offer Links

 

 

When shopping online, using online coupon codes or promo links is similar to using paper coupons or shopping sales at local stores. 

 


 

Online stores, on the other hand, have a working coupon code to enter during checkout that corresponds to a specific discount, and that amount is subtracted from the total purchase, as opposed to paper coupons that you hand to a cashier to redeem. The discounts range from a dollar off to free shipping and more.

 

How do customers locate online coupon codes?

Coupon codes were not commonly found on retailer websites when they first became popular. Instead, coupon codes appeared on related shopping websites to direct customers to the retailer's online store. Many retailers still offer coupons on related websites, but they also offer one or more coupon codes on their store's websites or in weekly emails to their customers.

The best way to find a coupon code is to type "coupon code" into your favourite search engine, such as Google. This will produce a list of websites that provide coupon codes. Shoppers can then visit the websites and compare the available coupon savings.

After deciding what they want to buy, shoppers can sometimes find coupon codes for specific stores. This is accomplished by entering the retailer's name and "coupon code" into a search engine. For example, if a shopper knew they wanted to buy a dress from Macy's, they could search for Macy's coupon codes by entering "Macy's Coupon Codes."

 

What exactly are "linked" coupons, and how do you use them?

A linked coupon does not include a coupon code to activate the promotion but rather a special link. When you click the link, you will be taken to the online store, where you will automatically receive the discount when you check out.

 

How do I use a promotional code?

There are a few different places to enter coupon codes depending on the online store. In some cases, the coupon code can be entered before checking out in the shopping cart. Occasionally, the coupon is entered on the order review page before checking out. Before making an online purchase, search for the "submit" area of the coupon code (also known as a promo code).

 

What if I can't find a place to enter the code?

Check the website for an answer to the question in the FAQ (frequently asked questions) section. It is frequently found in a website's "Customer Service" section. If you don't see it anywhere on the site, contact customer service and ask them to explain how to use coupon codes on the website.

 

How can I tell if a coupon code is valid?

Typically, the merchant will have a page that shows how much you are charged for the merchandise, how much is discounted because you entered a coupon code, and any shipping fees that may be applied. This should appear on an order page before you approve the charges. If you enter the coupon code and the discount does not appear on the order review page, contact customer service before completing the transaction.

 

How can I tell if a "linked promotion" is effective?

In most cases, a linked promotion will take you directly to the product being promoted at a discounted price or the page advertising the discount. If it doesn't, check your order page before accepting the transaction as a purchase you want.

 

What if the coupon code doesn't work?

Many online stores will omit an expiration date for a coupon code so that it can be closely monitored and quickly changed or discontinued. Any promotion may be changed or discontinued at any time by an online store. If a coupon does not work, it is best to try a different code.

 

Can I use online coupons in stores near me?

Unless the promotion specifically states otherwise, local stores are not required to honour online deals. Some stores will honour the offer if you print it and bring it to them as a courtesy. It all depends on the deal and the store, but it never hurts to inquire.

 

Can I use multiple coupons at once to get a better deal?

Most coupon codes state that only one promotion per transaction or per item purchased may be used. You can increase your savings even further by looking for a rebate on the item you purchased with a coupon code such as Croma coupons. Rebates on electronic devices, computers, software, and home improvement items are plentiful. If you can't find one online, check with the retailer's customer service department to see if one is available for the item you want to buy.
